About

My name is Pranav Balaji and I am an experienced Product Owner and Business Analyst. With 6+ years of industry experience, I believe I can bring a lot to the table for any company looking for someone who is organized, proactive and great at effective communication. When faced with a complex problem, I excel at breaking down that complexity into easier, more understandable bite sized user stories.

Thanks to my software development background, my role as the bridge between the business side and the technology side is quite important. I am able to understand the perspectives of both the business and tech side, while being able to communicate between the two to achieve our business needs. I did not have a manufacturing background when I transitioned to my most recent role as a product owner for General Motors' electric vehicle division, but I worked hard to quickly become the subject matter expert so that I could continue to be the best source for the business and developers to come to so that our product visions were always aligned.

I am currently finishing my Masters through John's Hopkins University. Some of my classes include: Big Data Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Complex Project Management, Simulation for Business Applications and many others that keep me prime and ready for my field.

I am a proud Indian American who has a passion for rock climbing, drumming, cooking and games. Currently training to get lead ready for outdoor sport climbing. I love rock and metal music and continue to pursue learning the drums. I cook every chance I get and I am a competitive person who loves to figure out game strategy whether it be chess and other board games, or video games.
